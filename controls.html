Changing torch light temperature color

Use the slider at the bottom of the demo page. Temperature color values go from 1500k to 15000k.
Switching between auto and manual torch light rotation modes

Use the button at the bottom of the demo page. If you return to auto mode from manual, the torch will continue its rotation from that angle step.

Changing the rotation angle (azimuth) of the torch light

Use the slider at the bottom of the demo page, in manual mode only. While the slider is being activated, the rotation angle will change in real time.
